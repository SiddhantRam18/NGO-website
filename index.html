Today's urgency of organisation

Pasaydan seva prakalp is playing a leading role in taking care of orphan and abandoned children,it is our duty to support the organisation with funding and advocacy. Funding can be used to provide children with basic necessities such as food, clothing, and shelter. This can help to ensure that they are able to live a healthy and comfortable life. Funding can be used to pay for the education of children. This can help to give them the skills and knowledge they need to succeed in life and achieve their full potential.
